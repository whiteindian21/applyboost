Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, the new limits take effect immediately. When downgrading, the change will apply at the end of your current billing cycle.

How does the free trial work?

Our free plan offers limited access to AI features so you can test the platform. No credit card is required to start with the free plan.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards through PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. After cancellation, you'll still have access to paid features until the end of your billing period.
